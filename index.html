The Discipline, Pre-Committed.

Risk limits are not suggestions. They are the architecture that makes compounding possible.

Per Trade

2–3%

Every position sized so the stop loss risks between 2% and 3% of current equity. Default stop: 10 points. Hard broker stop enforced.

Per Day

5% max

Daily cap is a hard stop. If reached, trading halts for the session. No exceptions.

Per Week

10% max

Weekly cap is a hard stop. If reached, trading halts for the week. No exceptions.

Circuit Breaker

Three consecutive non-adherent trades trigger a 48-hour trading pause. No override. The architecture protects the operator from himself.
